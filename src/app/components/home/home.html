Installation

When upgrading or installing a new HVAC system, we start with a full home assessment to size equipment correctly, protect airflow, and balance comfort room to room. Our team lays out options that fit your home and budget, from central air and heat pumps to ductless mini-splits, with clear timelines and transparent pricing.

We handle permits, smart thermostat integration, and final commissioning so your system launches efficiently from day one. Expect clean installs, detailed walkthroughs, and a setup ready for long-term performance.

Repair

Unexpected HVAC issues can disrupt your comfort and daily routine. Whether your unit isn’t blowing cold air, making unusual noises, or failing to start altogether, our technicians provide fast, reliable repair services.

We work with all major brands and models, using advanced diagnostic tools to pinpoint the issue and restore functionality quickly. With AI Home Solutions, you’ll never be left sweating in the summer or shivering in the winter.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to maximizing the life and efficiency of your heating and cooling systems. Our seasonal HVAC tune-ups include filter replacement, coil cleaning, refrigerant level checks, and a full performance inspection.

These proactive visits help prevent breakdowns, reduce energy bills, and keep your system running like new. With a maintenance plan from AI Home Solutions, peace of mind comes standard.
